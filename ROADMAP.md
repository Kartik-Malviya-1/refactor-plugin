## Principles

- **Discovery answers:** What exists?
- **Planning answers:** What should it become?
- **Migration answers:** What exactly will change?
- **Execution applies:** Safe, reversible, verified.
- **Optimization learns:** Continuous improvement.

Every stage is a review gate. Nothing advances without designer approval.
